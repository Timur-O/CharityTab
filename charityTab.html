    Red Cross

    The ICRC responds quickly and efficiently to help people affected by armed conflict. They also respond to disasters in conflict zones, because the effects of a disaster are compounded if a country is already at war. Their activities include: addressing sexual violence, building respect for the law, economic security, enabling people with disabilities & physical rehabilitation, health, helping detainees, humanitarian diplomacy, migrants, refugees, asylum seekers, water and habitat. Visit their website at https://www.icrc.org/.
